--- ABI Vehicle Data Glossary ---

ABI = Association of British Insurers
TVI = Thatcham Coding System

Kerb Weight = Boş ağırlık, insan veya eşya (yük) hesaba katılmadan ve akaryakıtı dolu olan bir 
		  aracın, taşınması zorunlu aletler ile birlikte toplam ağırlığı.

Transmission Type = Şanzıman tipi, elektrikli arabalarda yoktur (automatic, sequential automatic vs. şeklinde gösterilir)

Trim Level = Trim levels are used by manufacturers to identify a vehicle's level of equipment 
		 or special features

AEB Type = Automatic Emergency Breaking, safety feature

Enhanced Vehicle Description = Model + 
					Trim Level +
					Engine Label +
					(Power Output) +
					Number of Doors +
					Primary Fuel Type +
					Transmission Type +
					Drive Type +
					(Number of Seats)

ABI datasında Enhanced Vehicle Description'dan anladığım kadarıyla, araçları sınıflandırırken yukarıdaki
patern kullanılmış. Parantez içindekiler "her zaman kullanılmıyor" anlamına geliyor.




--- Technical Vehicle Data (KBA) Glossary ---

Displacement = Engine displacement is the combined swept volume of the pistons inside the cylinders
of an engine. It is calculated from the bore (diameter of the cylinders), stroke (distance the piston 
travels), and number of cylinders. Displacement is an important factor, as it has a direct impact on 
an engine’s power output, fuel efficiency, and in some countries, how a vehicle is taxed.
